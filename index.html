About Us

( WELCOME TO THE LITTLE BAKERY! THANKS SO MUCH FOR STOPPING BY )

I’m Deepika, the girl behind the counter and the camera. Ever since I can remember,baking has been that one thing that lights me up. There isn’t a day that goes by that I’m not thinking or dreaming about desserts. From cookies to cakes to bars to brownies, it’s my passion to share homemade dessert recipes that make you drool from just one photo.
